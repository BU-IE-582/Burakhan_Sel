Task 2

As a task 2, I choose to anaylze difference in scores and number of different odds on a single match. By this way, I would like to observe if the difference in scores is small, number of odds (including changing odds and different type of odds) is high. Small difference in scores intuitively means that powers of both teams are more or less equal and there is a high probability of draw. My hypothesis is that if there are so many changes in odds or many type of odds are provided for a game, the game will end with small difference in scores. In order to do that, I followed the steps that are shown below.

Chose a bookmaker which is Betclic for my case and filter the data by 1x2 bet type:

all_data_DT<-data.table(all_data)
betclic<-all_data_DT[betType=="1x2" & bookmaker=="Betclic"]

Define a new column for difference in scores:

betclic$score1 = as.numeric(lapply(strsplit(as.character(betclic$score), split=":"), "[", 1))
betclic$score2 = as.numeric(lapply(strsplit(as.character(betclic$score), split=":"), "[", 2))
betclic[,Score_diff:=abs(score1-score2)]

Compute the frequencies of MatchIds and merge with my data:

number_of_odds<-data.table(table(betclic$matchId))
names(number_of_odds)[1] <- "matchId"
data<-data.table(merge(number_of_odds,betclic,by="matchId"))

Eliminate dublicated MatchIds:

data<-unique(data,by="matchId")

Plot the difference in scores vs frequencies:

plot(data$Score_diff,data$N)

From this graph, high number of frequencies are generally for the games that have small score difference. If frequency of odds is low for a game, the game will probably end with a high score difference. On the contrary, if the frequeny of odds is high for a game, the game will probably end with a small score difference. Additionally, apart from the frequency, the number of games that the bookmaker provides odd is high for the games that ended with small score difference. It can be seen in the graph that, there are more points for the small score differences. This shows us that bets are generally provided for the games which result are more unpredictible.
